Al-Huriyya Project

Our modus operandi

  • We have a registration database where members are listed after filling out a form.
  • Members have the option of giving a continuous contribution of N250/week, N1000/month, N3000/quarter, N6000 twice a year, or N12000/year.
  • Monthly reminders are sent out via email and SMS whenever payment is due, 10 days to the last day of the month.
  • Members have the option of contributing on behalf of themselves or/and a loved one who has passed onThere will be reports sent to all members at the beginning and end of any project embarked on.
  • All money from the contribution is 100% used ONLY for the projects. Administrative costs (emails, SMSs, letters, adverts, etc) are not included.
  • Member can contribute towards administrative purposes seperately.
